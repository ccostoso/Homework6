GIPHY App

Instructions:

Select a button from the list at the top of the page to display 10 GIF images in the display window.

If you'd like to add your own animal to the list, create a new button by typing in the text box above and clicking "Submit"!

To animate an image, click on the image to make it move. To end animation, click on the image again.

NOTE: not all images can be animated.
